Who We Are

Friends of Fuo 2 Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit working to improve education in Fuo AME Zion Basic School in Tamale, Northern Ghana. We provide desks, books, and essential classroom supplies so students can learn in a safe, supportive, and dignified environment.

OUR HISTORY

Logo

Logo

Our journey began in 2023, when our founder met Mary Naa-Afi Kofi, a dedicated teacher, at a missionary conference in Accra.

Through her photos, we saw children learning on the floor or squeezed into broken desks. Inspired by the students’ determination and Mary’s unwavering commitment.

Friends of Fuo 2Inc. was created to help meet these needs, one classroom at a time. Today, the school seves 497 students from Nursery through Junior High School.

In addition to classroom support, we also help students from Ghana pursue higher education, expanding access and opportunity beyond the schoolyard. Together, we are building brighter futures through education, dignity, and care

OUR PURPOSE

OUR MISSION

Friends of Fuo 2 Inc. is dedicated to enhancing educational opportunities for students at Fuo AME Zion Basic School in Tamale, Ghana. We provide essential resources such as desks, books, and classroom supplies to create a conducive learning environment. Our mission is to empower students to reach their full potential through access to quality education and supporting students as they pursue higher education.

OUR VISION

Friends of Fuo 2 Inc. envisions a future where every student has the tools to thrive; From desks and classrooms materials to the support needed to pursue higher education. We believe that quality education should never be limited by resources, and that empowered students create stronger communities and brighter futures
