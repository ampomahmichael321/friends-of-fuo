OUR STORY

Our journey began in 2023 when our founder met Mary naa-Afi Kofi, a dedicated teacher, at a missinary conference in Accra.

Through her photots, we saw children learning on the floor or squeezed into broken desks.

Inspired by the students; determination and unwavering commitment, Friends of Fuo 2 nc was created to help meet these needs, one classroom at a time.

Today, the school serves 497 students from Nursery through Juniour High School.

In addidtion to classroom support, we also help students from Ghana pursue higher education, expanding access and opportunity beyond the schoolyard
Together, were building brighter futures through education, dignity, and care.
